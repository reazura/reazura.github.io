Preparation

  • Yggdrasil Seed, Berry, Premium White Potions
  • Slime pet and pet food

General

  • Stay at center circle and keep baphomet at center, only go away from center when doing mechanics
  • Keep healing up all the time, don't wait for priest

Shadow Baphomet Phase

3 shadow baphomet will spawn. The first baphomet you hit gives you a personal buff of the corresponding color (0:05), you may only damage baphomet matching with your buff.

Pull each shadow bapho away from tower and away from other shadow baphomets, then kill them.

Sickle Phase

Spread out and catch falling scythes. Explosion deals a fixed 166k damage, if they are all caught. Uncaught scythes deal additional dmg to everyone

Emperium Phase

Kill the purple emperium before the lava hell opens. Stand with the gold emperium to avoid lava

Look at your minimap to find where the emperium is. Always target the purple emperium on the left side.
